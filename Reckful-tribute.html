A Tribute to Byron Bernstein

The man behind Reckful

R.I.P. May 8, 1989 – July 2, 2020

A brief history of Reckful in gaming

  • Asherons call - He started his journey from a little-known game Asheron's Call in 1999 when he was only 10 years old. At that time he was known as Anti Parazi and soon he became one of the best players on the server as a legendary Pk'er (player killer).
  • World of Warcraft - In World of Warcraft, he finished rank one online for six seasons in a row, and never used spells that were deemed a "necessity" such as mutilate. He was the first player to reach 3000 rating and won Major League Gaming's World of Warcraft tournament in 2010. 1234
  • Twitch streaming - In 2011, Bernstein released the gaming movie Reckful 3. It reached one million views within a week (as of July 2020, the video has nearly six million views). He later won the WarcraftMovies top skilled contest, in which players cast their vote for player of the year. He began streaming on twitchtv and due to his legendary repuation became the most popular streamer on the site almost over night.
  • Hearthstone - He was also one of the first to play Hearthstone and even got a 3rd-4th place among streamers at Innkeeper's Invitational in 2013. One of the highlights of his career was the fail on Blizzcon 2013 Hearthstone Invitational which became a popular meme in the scene.
  • IRL streaming - Reckful was one of the first to show the potential of IRL streaming with the release of Pokemon GO. His streams were consecutive reaching viewer numbers of 30k+ and proved to the platform the potential of content outside desktop gaming.
  • Everland - Being an MMO gamer msot of his life Reckful understood the power of social interaction and connectivity in gaming. Wanting to give back he decided to found a game studio consisiting of members of his own community, togeather they set out to create his dream social world, Everland.
  • Legacy - Over the past year, he has streamed 738 hours, it’s 122 broadcasts in total. His Hours Watched reached the mark of 6,1 million. For the last year, 97K viewers subscribed to his channel, and the peak of the year was reached on June 19, 2019 - 33964 simultaneous viewers.

Reckful after a lifetime of battling Bipolar disorder committed suicide at the age of 31, he revolutionised streaming and his contributions to gaming will not be forgotten.
